Site Name

Kweneng District Council

The name represent an area in Botswana that have been mapped and allocated a council to take care of. The domain name for the site will be kweneng.org

Purpose

Our purpose on developing this website is to ensure that the public is aware of the services that the council provide. The site will provide information of the leadership of the council and the contacts the puplic can use to contact the council. The site also will provide other upcoming events that the public should be aware of. The target audience to this website is the public, anyone who need service from the council.

Scenarios

  • Which villages and settlements are part of Kwening District ?
  • What is the contact number of the council ?
  • Who are the leadership of the council ?
  • Which villages of the council are available to apply for residential plot ?
  • Which area in the council is available to apply for plough plots ?
  • What future events are planned by the council ?
